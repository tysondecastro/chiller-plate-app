What is Randevū?

What is our vision?

Randevū is the place to be for all meet ups and community events.


The Problem. We believe that there is a fundemental problem today, with human interactions. We believe that humans are spending too much time on the phones and computers, becasue of this, we have created a platform for users to get the needed and deserved human interactions in the their communites.

Wellness. Users can promote a healthy lifestyle by attending local events in their communities. We provide users with the opportunity to get active with others in your community through fun pick up games, group hikes, and other physical activities.

Community Users can both make new friends and come together with other members of their community. Through our unique approach in finding fun activities near you, anyone can meet the people who make up the place that makes up their home.
