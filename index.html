Randevū

Randevū is a platform for users to build positive relationships in their local communities. People can meet others who share a similar desire to achieve a healthy lifestyle, get active, and step out of their comfort zones.

Our Mission

Hawaiiʻs staggering health statistics show that people in Hawaii are at serious risk for Type 2 diabetes, obesity, cardiac diseases, and stroke. As people of active lifestyles, we value the importance of helping our lāhui (community) rise above the current status quo through Randevū: A way to connect with others who share the same desires and passions.

What Makes Us Unique

Randevū helps its users to kickstart healthy lifestyles in their own community. From pick up games at local parks to walking groups right in your neighborhood, we foster the creation of individuals motivated to live their best lives.
